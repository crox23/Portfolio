A little bit about me.

I am currently a student at UT- Austin for AI and ML application. I am also attaining a Professional Certificate in MERN Full-Stack development from MIT. This year the COVID19 impacted the lives of many, and I took part in EuvsVirus of the European Union Commission, winning as Trust in Science project. I am working on one more social impact project about how to integrate very different expertise on COVID19, applying Complexity Theory through AI, Operational research, and Cognitive science.

As the manager of 40+ employees, I am a self-driven person, goals oriented, and well-organized. People close to me define me as a creative problem solver and a very curious person that makes things happen. I am a Tech and Startup lover. I do really love the start-up ecosystem, especially the underlining energy of bringing to life the ideas and make things happen. I am an enthusiast about Coursera, Edx, Y-Combinator, 500 Startups, Tech Crunch, etc.
